About Our Community

Application Library Engineering group is an open platform where developers of software application, libraries and components can come together and interact with an intent to learn, collaborate and contribute towards a fast-paced and enriched application development ecosystem for connected consumer devices.

One As All, All As One

HarmonyOs is a next-gen operating system that can run on a wide range of smart devices. It enables different smart devices to speak the same language, facilitating better connection and collabration, and bringing a simple, smooth, continuous, secure and reliable interaction experience in all senarios.
